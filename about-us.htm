About Nada Sadhana

Sadguru Saint Kabir Saheb has described about Surat Shabda yoga which is called Nadanu sadhana(Nada Sadhana) in his scripture. According to his philosophy, by the Surat Shabda yoga salvation can be attained.

Chhuli Gachhadar had donated some land to Manikchand Das seventy years ago. At that land Kabir sthan had been established. Some saint has been managing this Kabir sthan. Under managing of Swami Murali Dharanand sahib, Nada Sadhana Prakash Peeth has Established on 2063 B.S. through Kabir Sthan by the grace of initiator H.H. Mahant Prakash Mani Saheb.

Main aim of Nada Sadhana Prakash Peeth is to give initiation of Nada Sadhana and to lift in the deep experience of Nada for National and International people. This Nada Shadhana generates experience of Fourth State (Turiya) which is called Transcendental State . It is necessary of institute to provide education and initiation of Nada Sadhana.

Research of mystic or Divine melody Anachat Nada is a kind of sound which inaudible by our ears and it can’t be uttered by any voice So this is a mystical and metaphysical or divine melody sound. It has been producing and vibrating for 24 hours in all beings without any hurts and flections. He only can hear this sound who has in warded attitude.

सम्पूर्ण चिन्ता र सम्पूर्ण चेष्टा (प्रयास) छोडेर नादलाई नै अनुसन्धान (श्रवण) गर्नु पर्छ जसले योगमा साम्राज्य अर्थात् अधिकार प्राप्त गर्न चाहन्छ । यसै नाद साधनाको परम्परा लुप्त भएको अवस्थामा पुन: जागृत गर्नको लागि यस संस्थाको अग्रिम भूमिका हुनेछ ।
यस नाद साधनाको धारा प्रवाह सम्पूर्ण प्राणीमा भए पनि मनुष्य मात्रले अनुभव गर्न सक्दछ । तसर्थ यो साधनाको विषयमा विशेष जानकारी प्राप्त गर्नको लागि विशेषज्ञ सद्गुरुको आवश्यक छ ।
